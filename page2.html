IKEA TOMORROW

IKEA is known for its vision to deliver great design that improves the everyday life of someone who loves afforable, reliable, functional, and forward-thinking products. In the year 2086, IKEA decided to experiment with different styles of furniture by collaborating with a wide-range of artists and designers for their new program, "IKEA TOMORROW".

As apart of our capsule collection, we created an installation in Almhult,Sweden, showcasing our proposal,'Room 2086', for "IKEA TOMORROW". For this capsule, we designed a grid pattered rug, octogon shaped coffee table, concrete couch, and chair-bench-sectional hybrid with desk attached. We experimented with sillouette, material, and color palette in our design of essential home furniture.
